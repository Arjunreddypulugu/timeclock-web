Common Solutions

Based on your app's architecture, here are some things to try:

  1. Check browser console for exact errors (this page captures them above)
  2. Make sure all dependencies in package.json are installed and compatible
  3. Verify that @emotion/styled and styled-components aren't conflicting
  4. Look for MUI theme related issues - your ErrorMessage component was recently changed
  5. Verify your API connection is working properly
